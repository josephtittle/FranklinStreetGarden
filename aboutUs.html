The Garden

The goal is to bring the community together to not only receive produce, but also to learn how to reap the benefits of the work put into it. All the garden needs is some helping hands, a willing heart, and a love for the outdoors. You do not need to know anything about gardening to be a part of this community garden. We are happy to have anyone that would like to be a part of it!

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a positive environment for everyone. in a bustling city, we want our community to enjoy produce and the peaceful tranquility of a flourishing flower and produce garden.

"To plant a garden is to believe in tomorrow."
Audrey Hepburn

Helping Hands

The garden is has 14 raised bed. Gardening gloves, water, and the necessary tools are provided. Franklin Street Events Association has graciously helped the garden become what it is today. We would love to have new volunteers to enjoy the delicious vegetables and admire the beauty that comes with it! We can teach you anything you would like to know!
